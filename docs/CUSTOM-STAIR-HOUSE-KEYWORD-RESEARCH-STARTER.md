# Custom Stair House Keyword Research Starter

## Purpose
This is a practical first-pass keyword strategy for Custom Stair House.

The goal is **not** to chase broad traffic.
The goal is to rank for **high-intent commercial searches** tied to:
- custom stair treads
- stair nosings
- vent covers
- exact-match / matching accessories
- flooring-store and installer relevance
- Northern Virginia / Washington DC area demand

This should guide:
- homepage positioning
- page targeting
- title/meta direction
- internal links
- future content expansion

---

## Strategic Thesis
The SEO play here should be:

### 1. Commercial-page first
Prioritize pages tied directly to products and buying intent:
- homepage
- stair treads
- stair nosings
- vent covers
- for flooring stores
- request a quote
- service area

### 2. Exact-match positioning
The strongest differentiator is not generic wood products.
It is:
- matching accessories
- customer-provided flooring material
- exact-match quality
- cleaner finished result
- fewer stain-match headaches

### 3. Narrow local authority
Do not try to become a broad flooring blog.
Build topical authority around:
- stair accessories
- wood flooring accessory matching
- exact-match treads / nosings / vents
- flooring trade buyer support
- Northern Virginia / DC geography

---

## What to Avoid
Avoid building the site around broad vanity terms like:
- flooring
- hardwood flooring
- stair remodeling
- stair design
- woodwork
- carpentry
- custom woodworking

Why:
- too broad
- weak purchase intent for this business
- invites the wrong audience
- harder to rank
- less aligned with the actual offer

So the right approach is:
- use AI for structure and synthesis
- use SEO tools later for validation if needed
