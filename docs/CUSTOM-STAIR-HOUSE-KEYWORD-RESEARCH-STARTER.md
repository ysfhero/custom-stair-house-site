# Custom Stair House Keyword Research Starter

## Purpose
This is a practical first-pass keyword strategy for Custom Stair House.

The goal is **not** to chase broad traffic.
The goal is to rank for **high-intent commercial searches** tied to:
- custom stair treads
- stair nosings
- vent covers
- exact-match / matching accessories
- flooring-store and installer relevance
- Northern Virginia / Washington DC area demand

This should guide:
- homepage positioning
- page targeting
- title/meta direction
- internal links
- future content expansion

---

## Strategic Thesis
The SEO play here should be:

### 1. Commercial-page first
Prioritize pages tied directly to products and buying intent:
- homepage
- stair treads
- stair nosings
- vent covers
- for flooring stores
- request a quote
- service area

### 2. Exact-match positioning
The strongest differentiator is not generic wood products.
It is:
- matching accessories
- customer-provided flooring material
- exact-match quality
- cleaner finished result
- fewer stain-match headaches

### 3. Narrow local authority
Do not try to become a broad flooring blog.
Build topical authority around:
- stair accessories
- wood flooring accessory matching
- exact-match treads / nosings / vents
- flooring trade buyer support
- Northern Virginia / DC geography

---

## What to Avoid
Avoid building the site around broad vanity terms like:
- flooring
- hardwood flooring
- stair remodeling
- stair design
- woodwork
- carpentry
- custom woodworking

Why:
- too broad
- weak purchase intent for this business
- invites the wrong audience
- harder to rank
- less aligned with the actual offer

## Recommendation on Trim / Transition / Moulding Terms
Yes — these terms should be added to the strategy, but with the right role.

### Best use:
- secondary keywords
- body copy support
- FAQ language
- image alt text
- future supporting pages

### Why:
- **transition** terms are especially strong because they often signal a real product need and active buying intent
- **trim** terms are broader and can help widen discoverability, especially when paired with words like `hardwood`, `wood flooring`, `laminate`, or `matching`
- **moulding/molding** terms are useful because many buyers use them interchangeably with nosing, transitions, and trim

### Important caution:
Do **not** let generic words like `trim` or `moulding` replace the stronger core page focus on:
- stair treads
- stair nosings
- vent covers
- exact-match accessories

Use these added terms to widen semantic coverage, not to dilute the main positioning.

## Bottom Line
The winning SEO strategy for Custom Stair House is:
- narrow
- commercial
- exact-match focused
- trade-aware
- local
- semantically broad enough to capture trim / transition / moulding searches without becoming generic

Do not try to win the whole flooring internet.
Win the exact category this business is built for.
